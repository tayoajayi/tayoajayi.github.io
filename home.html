About Me


I am an optimization and data science researcher currently working at Nature Source Improved Plants. I have extensive experience modeling in several different applications, including biology, healthcare, and sports analytics. I have also conducted basic research in optimization. I graduated from Yale University with a BS in Applied Mathematics, and I earned my PhD from Rice University's Computational and Applied Mathematics Departmet with advisor Andrew Schaefer.
